Rules


Spymaster rules

Each turn, the spymaster may only give a SINGLE WORD clue to their team followed by the number of cards they expect their team to guess. For example, if the spymaster wants their team to guess two cards saying "WALL" and "CHINA", the spymaster might say "panda, two".

The spymaster may not give any other clues or body signals to get their team to guess their team's hidden words and may not give hints or talk when it is not their turn.

Playing a turn

To make a guess, a team member must click a card on the board. If they reveal a card with their team's color (red or blue), they may go again. If they reveal a brown card or a card with the other team's color, it becomes the other team's turn.

Ending a turn

Once your team wishes to end its turn, click the "END TURN" button. If time runs out during your turn, a card is randomly selected and flipped over and it becomes the other team's turn. Beware - if the black assassin card is turned over during your team's turn, the other team automatically wins.

Ending the game

The game ends when all the cards for one team have been turned over OR when the assassin card has been revealed.
